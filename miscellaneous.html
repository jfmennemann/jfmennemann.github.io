Optimal Control of Bose Einstein Condensates
Jan-Frederik Mennemann

Abstract. In this seminar-talk we consider optimal control of Bose-Einstein condensates in magnetic microtraps. The mean-field dynamics of such condensates is well described by the Gross-Pitaevkii equation, which is an example of a nonlinear Schr√∂dinger equation. We define the objective of the control, derive the optimality system and show how to solve state and adjoint equation with a time-splitting spectral method. Finally, we employ different solvers for the minimum search to compute an optimal control, which steers the condensate between an initial state to a desired state at final time T.
